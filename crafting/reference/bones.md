# Desecration bones (Well of Souls)

> Generated by `pnpm reference` from poe2db.tw. Do not hand-edit — re-run to refresh.
> Exact effect text is datamined; prices live in `data/economy`.

Bones add a desecrated modifier; the bone's **minimum modifier level** sets the floor of what can be revealed. Gnawed caps item level (low), Preserved has no floor, Ancient = min mod level 40 (high tiers only). Jawbone/Collarbone/Rib/Cranium select the slot pool.

| Bone | Min mod level | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Gnawed Jawbone | — | Desecrates a Rare Weapon or Quiver |
| Gnawed Collarbone | — | Desecrates a Rare Amulet, Ring or Belt |
| Gnawed Rib | — | Desecrates a Rare Armour |
| Preserved Jawbone | — | Desecrates a Rare Weapon or Quiver |
| Preserved Collarbone | — | Desecrates a Rare Amulet, Ring or Belt |
| Preserved Rib | — | Desecrates a Rare Armour |
| Preserved Cranium | — | Desecrates a Rare Jewel |
| Ancient Jawbone | 40 | Desecrates a Rare Weapon or Quiver |
| Ancient Collarbone | 40 | Desecrates a Rare Amulet, Ring or Belt |
| Ancient Rib | 40 | Desecrates a Rare Armour |
