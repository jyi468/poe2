These are the spawn weights every bow-craft EV number derives from. A modifier's **tier
required level** (`T@N`) is what the level-floor exalts gate on: an **Exalted Orb** can roll
any tier, **Greater Exalted** only tiers with required level ≥ 35, **Perfect Exalted** only
≥ 50. So a higher exalt guarantees a higher *tier* but also shrinks the pool — changing the
per-slam *type* odds below.

| Exalted Orb | 87.2% | 7.0% | 1.6% | 14.1% |
| Greater Exalted (min 35) | 86.4% | 3.6% | 3.6% | 5.2% |
| Perfect Exalted (min 50) | 90.4% | 2.4% | 2.4% | 2.4% |

> Note the trade-off: crit's share *falls* as the floor rises (its high tiers are a small
> slice of the high-tier pool), but every crit you do land is a better tier. **Greater Exalt
> (min 35)** is the sweet spot for crit ≥T3; **Perfect (min 50)** forces T2+ but is rarer.
